Our Founding History:

The MMA gym was established in 2008 by a group of like-minded individuals who shared a common vision: to create a space where individuals of all ages and backgrounds could come together to learn, train, and grow through the art of mixed martial arts. The founders, each with a unique set of skills and experiences, pooled their resources to create a gym that would not only produce top-notch fighters but also foster a sense of camaraderie and support among its members.

In the early days, The MMA gym started in a humble space with a few mats, a makeshift ring, and a lot of heart. As word spread about the gym's commitment to excellence, the membership grew, and so did the facilities. Today, The MMA gym boasts state-of-the-art training equipment, world-class coaches, and a thriving community that spans beyond the walls of our facility.

Our gym is more than just a place to train; it's a family. We've witnessed the transformation of individuals who walked through our doors for the first time, unsure of their potential, and watched them evolve into confident, skilled martial artists. The success stories of our members fuel our passion and drive to continuously improve and provide the best possible environment for growth.

At The MMA gym, we offer a comprehensive training program that combines the best elements of various martial arts disciplines. Whether you're a seasoned fighter or a beginner looking to get in shape, our diverse classes cater to all skill levels. From Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu to Muay Thai, boxing to wrestling, our expert coaches are here to guide you on your journey to becoming the best version of yourself.

Coach Conor McGregor - Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Specialist:

Coach McGregor is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt with over 12 years of experience in the art. His journey began at a young age, and his passion for BJJ has taken him to competitions around the world. Known for his technical expertise and attention to detail, Coach McGregor brings a wealth of knowledge to our gym. Whether you're a beginner learning the fundamentals or an advanced practitioner looking to fine-tune your skills, Coach McGregor is dedicated to helping you reach your full potential on the mat.

Coach Tyron Woodley - Muay Thai Maestro:

Coach Woodley, our resident Muay Thai expert, is a seasoned fighter with a decorated career in the striking arts. With a focus on traditional Muay Thai techniques combined with modern training methods, Coach Woodley is committed to honing the striking skills of our members. From mastering the art of eight limbs to developing strategic fight plans, Coach Woodley brings a dynamic and challenging approach to every training session. His energy and enthusiasm inspire fighters to push their limits and embrace the beauty of Muay Thai.

Coach Hasbulla Magomedov - Boxing and Wrestling Guru:

Coach Magomedov is a former national champion in both boxing and wrestling, bringing a dual expertise to our gym. His knowledge of footwork, head movement, and takedowns is unparalleled, making him a valuable asset to fighters seeking a well-rounded skill set. Coach Magomedov instills the principles of discipline and perseverance in every training session, empowering our members to overcome challenges both inside and outside the ring. Whether you're a striker looking to improve your boxing or a grappler aiming to enhance your wrestling game, Coach Magomedov has the experience and passion to guide you to success.
